About Me My career path has encompassed a variety of fields, including material science, architecture and industrial design. I've done research on the fabrication of thin film, low temperature aluminum superconductors under Professor Nina Markovic, Architectural design at PRESENT Architecture and product design with additive manufacturing at Studio Bitonti. I've led innovation based projects involving the additive production of wearable technology, the manufacturing of polymer compounds and the generation of blended auxetic structures. I've also led a variety of design projects, including prosthetic leg covers, 3D printed footwear a a scoliosis brace that is now in the permanent collection at the Cooper Hewitt Design Museum.

My sincere belief is that a design is only as good as the concept that ignited it, and the best concepts originate at the intersection between multiple fields. To reach me directly, please email me at wildfeuer.peter@gmail.com
