UPPKOPLA
KEYS
In 2019, I was hired to work with IKEA, Unyq and Carbon 3D on another project for gaming ergonomics. This time the focus was on customized gaming accessories for mass production that can improve player performance.
Press Release: Uppkoppla






For this new type of product line, we wanted a modern, but sleek aesthetic that was unique to its age. Visual research, lent itself to the idea that texture is the new signage. Textures don't just illustrate function but can also facilitate it. By utilizing 3D textures, applications for grip enhancement, sweat management and air flow are all possible in one aesthetic device.









Gaming ergonomics have existed in console controllers for a very long time but have not carried over into desktop applications. By studying gameplay in First Person Shooters we found there was an opportunity to apply the same ergonomic concept to counterstrike by introducing textures onto the keys that control player movement.




Depending on the player's gameplay and aesthetic preferences I built algorithms to generate thousands of different iterations, and made a series of "presets" that players could choose from or customize.





Using python and javascript I made a mock web application for users to customize their keys and send a clean printable mesh file directly to our manufacturing site, creating a fully automated process.




The new keys were presented at the 2019 IKEA Democratic Design Days in Almhult, Sweden, and gaming event in Poland.
