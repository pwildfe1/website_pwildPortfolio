In 2019, I was hired to work with IKEA, Unyq and Carbon 3D on another project for gaming ergonomics. This time the focus was on customized gaming accessories for mass production that can improve player performance.
Press Release: Uppkoppla






Typical office solutions for wrist supports don't apply here as they focus static solutions that don't address sweat management, air flow and user fit. Instead, by introducing a 3D lattice that varies in structure, we can offer customized fit, support, cushioning and airflow in one simple solution.






Our initial ideas focused on enabling movement for gameplay. Later iterations introduced varying lattice structures to add cushioning beneath the wrist and support at its sides.




The support was built algorithmically, meaning it could easily adapt to fit any user profile for perfect fit based on two photos of the users arm





The new final prototypes were presented at a live demonstration during IKEA Democratic Design Days in Almhult, Sweden.
