UBIK IKEA

The gaming world is a large growing market that is constantly looking for new products to improve performance; however, many of the products available to them now fail to address their unique ergonomic problems. IKEA wanted to address these problems and include a customized component in their solution. We were hired to design a new type of gaming chair that suited the gaming worlds needs.
Press Release: Ubik Stool






Unlike office workers, esports players sit at the edge of their seat with the screen placed drastically closer to their face. Therefore, typical lumbar solutions for office chairs are not applicable. Instead, if we focus on rotating the pelvis we can achieve the same lumbar and cervical solutions without changing the player's relationship with his/her screen.




Precedents exist for this type of solution, namely saddle stools. These seats focus on dipping the legs forward to rotate the pelvis and correct the spine.




The final design included 3D cushions that could be customized to fit the player's physical profile and gamer style.




Rather than working from scratch, we built the new assembly to fit with existing IKEA parts, creating a stool that could be brought to market quickly at a relatively low cost.




The new chair concept was presented at IKEA Democratic Design Days in Almhult, Sweden.
